Everything You Need

Built to make grievance handling fast, fair, and transparent

AI Classification

Gemini AI automatically categorizes your grievance — Lab, Infrastructure, Discipline, and more — instantly.

Smart Routing

Grievances are automatically sent to the right authority — HOD, Warden, or Admin — without manual intervention.

Image Evidence

Upload photos as evidence. AI analyzes images to detect violence, damage, or cleanliness issues automatically.

Priority System

High priority grievances trigger instant email alerts. Medium priority grievances appear in the dashboard queue.

Academic coordinators review and approve grievances before they reach authorities, ensuring quality control.

Live Dashboard
